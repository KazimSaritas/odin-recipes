Bruschetta

Description

Bruschetta is a simple Italian appetizer made of toasted bread topped with a fresh mixture of diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar.

Ingredients

  • 1 Baguette Sliced
  • 4 Ripe Tomatoes
  • 2 Garlic Clobes, Minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Toast the sliced baguette until golden and crisp.
  2. Mix diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Spoon the tomato mixture onto the toasted bread and serve immediately.
