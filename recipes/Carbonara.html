Bruschetta

Description

Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, Parmesan, pancetta, and black pepper.

It's a simple and delicious recipe that's perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 4 oz pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti until al dente and take it out, reserving some pasta water.
  2. Cook pancetta until crispy in the pan.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan together in a bowl.
  4. Toss the hot pasta with the pancetta, then quickly stir in the egg mixture, adding pasta water if needed for creaminess.
  5. Season with black pepper and serve.
