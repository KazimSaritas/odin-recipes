Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is an Italian layered pasta dish, typically made with sheets of pasta, meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and melted cheese, baked until golden and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • basil
  • Oregano
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook ground meat with onions and garlic, then add tomato sauce and simmer.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles until al dente.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan in a bowl.
  5. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat layers, finishing with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove foil and bake another 20-25 minutes until golden.
  9. Let it rest before serving.
